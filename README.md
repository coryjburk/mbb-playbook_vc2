# MBB Consulting Interview Playbook — User Manual

**Full-Time MBA Program · David Eccles School of Business · Consulting Track**

A browser-based training suite for McKinsey, Bain, and BCG interview preparation. No installation, no account, no login — everything runs in your browser.

**Start here:** **[Intv Playbook - MBB Consulting (vC2)](https://coryjburk.github.io/mbb-playbook_vg/)**

---

## 1. Requirements

| Item | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Browser (reading/study) | Any modern browser |
| Browser (voice practice) | **Chrome or Edge** — the speech-to-text engine is not supported in Firefox and is unreliable in Safari |
| Microphone | Required for voice practice; the browser will ask for permission the first time |
| Internet | Required to load pages and for speech transcription; your practice data itself never leaves your browser unless you paste it somewhere |
| Account | None |

Your dashboard scores are stored **locally in the browser you use**. They do not sync between devices, browsers, or incognito windows. Use the same browser on the same computer throughout your prep.

## 3. The Core Training Loop

This is the workflow the whole playbook is built around:

1. **Open a sprint** and pick a case or question.
2. **Read the prompt, then click "Practice Case Opening Remarks" / "Practice Live Verbalization"** to open the Practice Arena.
3. **Click 🎙️ Start Spoken Answer** and deliver your answer out loud. Your speech is transcribed live into the text box. A **2:00 countdown** starts with the recording (see §4).
4. **Click 🛑 Stop Recording** when finished.
5. **Click "Generate Rigorous AI Coaching Prompt."** The page packages your transcript together with the case's ideal solution path into a grading prompt.
6. **Copy the payload** and paste it into your AI assistant of choice (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.). You'll get structured feedback plus a **Dashboard Scorecard Matrix** — a score of 1–4 on each of the 8 competencies.
7. **Return to the Landing Hub → MBB Readiness Dashboard**, paste the scorecard into the **Import AI Scorecard** box, and click **Import scores**. All 8 metrics update at once.
8. **Repeat**, prioritizing whichever competencies score lowest.

## 4. The Practice Arena (Voice Tool)

- **Live transcript.** Only finalized speech segments are written to the box, so text appears in bursts a second or two behind you. That's normal.
- **Pauses are fine.** If you stop talking to think, the speech engine quietly restarts itself in the background — you do not need to press anything, and your transcript is preserved. Recording continues until *you* press Stop.
- **Filler Words counter.** Counts live occurrences of: *um, uh, like, basically, actually, you know, sort of, kind of.* Note that "like" and "actually" are counted every time they appear, including legitimate uses — treat the number as a trend indicator across attempts, not an absolute grade.
- **Timer.** Starts at 2:00 when recording begins (the standard budget for a case opening). It turns gold at 30 seconds remaining and red once you're over time, then counts how far over you went. Going over doesn't stop the recording.
- **Keyboard.** Press **Escape** to close the Practice Arena at any time.

## 5. The Readiness Dashboard

Found on the Landing Hub under **MBB Readiness Dashboard**.

**8 tracked competencies:** Case Structuring · Business Judgment · Math Accuracy · Synthesis · Fit / PEI Storytelling · Executive Presence · Communication Clarity · Recommendation Strength.

**Rubric tiers:**

| Tier | Band | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Emerging | 0.0–1.9 | Understands concepts but lacks consistent framework structure |
| 2. Developing | 2.0–2.9 | Structural promise, struggles with advanced synthesis |
| 3. Interview-Ready | 3.0–3.6 | Handles complex cases and behavioral prompts smoothly |
| 4. MBB-Caliber | 3.7–4.0 | Pristine MECE customization and senior partner presence |

**Two ways to update scores:**

- **Import (recommended):** paste the Dashboard Scorecard Matrix section from your AI feedback into the import box and click **Import scores**. Lines like `Case Structuring: 3. Interview-Ready` are detected automatically; bracketed or bolded variants also work.
- **Manual:** set each metric's dropdown yourself.

Metrics set to **N/A** are excluded from your aggregate score. **Reset all scores** wipes the dashboard clean if you want a fresh start.

## 6. Suggested 4-Week Plan

Full detail is on the Landing Hub under **4-Week Preparation Plan**. In brief:

- **Week 1 — Foundations.** Profit trees, behavioral story bank (4 finalized PEI stories), Math Drills 1–5 aloud daily, Sprint 1 openings.
- **Week 2 — Growth & Analytics.** Market entry, pricing, exhibit interpretation using the Sprint 2 and Sprint 5 Data/Exhibit tabs, Math Drills 6–10 daily.
- **Week 3 — M&A & Operations.** Due diligence, synergies, bottleneck logic, Math Drills 11–15, heavy verbalization across Sprints 2–3. Target: 15 recorded transcripts with filler words eliminated.
- **Week 4 — Mock & Partner Round.** Random sprint selection, candidate-led summaries, the 10 partner-round prompts, defending recommendations under pushback. Target: stable MBB-Caliber ratings across all 8 dashboard metrics.

## 7. Data & Privacy

- Everything on these pages is **client-side**. There is no server, no database, and no analytics collecting your practice data.
- Dashboard scores live in your browser's localStorage. **Clearing browsing data / site data will erase them.** They are also invisible to any other browser, device, or incognito window.
- Your spoken transcript leaves the page **only when you copy the coaching payload and paste it into an external AI tool.** Whatever you paste is then governed by that tool's data policies — keep highly personal details (health, finances, identifying information about others) out of your behavioral practice answers.

## 8. Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Cause & fix |
|---|---|
| "Requires Chrome/Edge browser speech engine" alert | You're in Firefox or another unsupported browser. Switch to Chrome or Edge for voice practice; all reading features still work anywhere. |
| "Microphone access is blocked" alert | Mic permission was denied. Click the lock/settings icon in the address bar, allow microphone access for the site, and restart the recording. |
| Transcript lags behind my speech | Normal — only finalized segments are written. Speak at a natural pace and let it catch up. |
| Recording button says Stop but nothing transcribes | Check the mic permission first; if it's allowed, check your internet connection (transcription requires it), then Stop and Start again. |
| My dashboard scores disappeared | You're in a different browser, device, profile, or incognito window than where you saved them — or browsing data was cleared. Scores are local to one browser. |
| Import says "No metric lines recognized" | Paste the **Dashboard Scorecard Matrix** section itself, with lines in the form `Case Structuring: 3. Interview-Ready`. If your AI reworded the metric names, fix them to match the 8 competency names exactly. |
| Filler count seems high | "Like" and "actually" count even when used legitimately. Compare counts across your own attempts rather than aiming for zero. |
